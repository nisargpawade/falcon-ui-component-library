Card

Cards are a convenient means of displaying content composed of different types of objects.

Simple Card

Use class card card-img-container card-img card-main card-text card-btns to get the above result.

Product Name

Company Name

Product description and other text


                <div class="card margin-1">
                  <div class="card-img-container">
                    <img class="card-img" src="..." alt="product img"/>
                  </div>
                  <div class="card-main padding-1">
                    <div class="card-text margin-l-1">
                      <p><b>Product Name</b></p>
                      <small>Company Name</small>
                      <p class="p-small">Product description and other text</p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="card-btns">
                      <button class="btn">Buy</button>
                      <button class="btn btn-ol">Add to cart</button>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                </div>
